REVIEWER: Sam Lakes


Name: Lotus


ADDRESS: 1131 Broad St, San Luis Obispo, CA 93401


CITY: San Luis Obispo


FOOD: 4.5


SERVICE: 4


VENUE: 5


RATING: 4.5


WRITTEN REVIEW:


Lotus is a Vietnamese restaurant located in the scenic town of San Luis Obispo.  It has a variety of cheap and expensive food and a generous selection of appetizers.  


Unfortunately, I have never had Vietnamese food so I do not have a basis for comparison.  With that being said, I really enjoyed the food here.  It was (for lack of a better word) delicious!  I ordered the Combination Pho which had your typical beef alongside some other parts of the cow.  I was hesitant at first but ended up enjoying it a lot.  


The service was pretty good.  The waiter was quick to take our orders, bring our food out, and asked us several times how our meals were turning out.  He was incredibly professional and nice.  I even asked his opinion between the two different types of Pho that I was considering and he gave me a detailed response to try and help me make an informed decision.  


The venue was downright adorable.  Due to the heat of the past few days we decided to eat outside (It was still hot inside of the restaurant at 7pm) and the atmosphere was nice.  It had quaint little tables and some (what I assume are) traditional Vietnamese decorations.  It is located in downtown SLO and we were lucky enough to get a close parking spot right across from the restaurant.  Parking in SLO is notoriously bad but you can occasionally get lucky.  However, I can't fault the owners of the restaurant for deciding to put a restaurant in one of the busiest parts of town.  IT is simple business.  Overall, the restaurant was fantastic and I definitely plan on going again in the near future.
