NAME: California Pizza Kitchen

ADDRESS: 9301 Tampa Avenue

CITY: Northridge

FOOD: 4

SERVICE: 5

VENUE: 3

RATING: 4

WRITTEN REVIEW:

California Pizza Kitchen is a pizza chain that serves California style pizza, sandwhiches, salads, and other items. The store I visited is located in Northridge just around Chatsworth where I worked this summer. A coworker and I ended up frequenting this location. The inside of the restaurant looked standard compared to picture of other CPKs. The location surrounding the restaurant is decent.Â 

Our service was great the first time we visted, and great every other time as well. Although it took a while to be seated, this is because the store was always pretty full. The servers were very polite and offered great alternatives for items they did not serve.

Before visiting this location, I was not a fan of CPK. I had been once before when I was younger and complained that the pizza didn't taste like other pizza (obviously not, every place's pizza tastes different, but I was under the impression that pizza was exactly what Pizza Hut made). This visit changed my opinion completely. The pizza was much better than I remembered. I ordered the pepperonni, which comes with oregano. I quickly realized this must have been what caused me to dislike the food when I was younger.

I have to say I really enjoyed the pizza and service. I have added a new location to my list of possible dining options. Hopefully other locations will have the same quality of food and service.
