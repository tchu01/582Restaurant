REVIEWER: Joey Wilson

NAME: Del's Pizzeria & Italian Restaurant

ADDRESS: 401 Shell Beach Rd

CITY: Pismo Beach

FOOD: 5

SERVICE: 4

VENUE: 3

RATING: 4

WRITTEN REVIEW: The food at Del's was delicious. I ordered a veggie calzone 

that was excellent. The mix of vegetables and cheese was a great combination.


The service at Del's was good. The waiter got us extra bread when we asked.

He also refilled the water at a timely interval.


The venue was located next to 101 in Pismo Beach. The views of the ocean were

excellent but only available from a few tables in the restaurant, this is why 

the rating is not higher.


Overall Del's gives great Italian food at a fair price, in a pleasant

location. I would give Del's a shot if you're looking for a nice dinner.
