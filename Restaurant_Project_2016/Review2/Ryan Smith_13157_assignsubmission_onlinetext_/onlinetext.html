REVIEWER: Ryan Smith


NAME: Milestone Tavern


ADDRESS: 972 Foothill Boulevard


CITY: San Luis Obispo


FOOD: 2


SERVICE: 4


VENUE: 4


RATING: 4


WRITTEN REVIEW:


The food at Milestone is both expensive and variable. The group that I went with

 ordered multiple orders of the same dish, yet were prepared differently. My own

 food was satisfactory (we all ordered the soft pretzel and sliders), however, o

ne of my friend's prezels was soggy and unappetizing. 


Despite the lackluster food, the service was exemplary. Although one of our wait

ers had to leave, he was replaced with a waitress that was equally as enthusiast

ic about recommending beer on tap and food options. Aside from that, the staff w

as fun to talk to and comfortable to have around.


Both the location and the setup for watching sports is optimal. There are enough TV's to show all the relevant sports from the area. The only drawback that I came across is that the location is apart from almost every other sports bar (aside from Charlies), and not easily accessible from most locations.


Overall, I would say that Milestone is a friendly environment that is accomadati

ng of all patrons. Despite the food being lackluster, it seems the location and

setup in the restaurant make up for that. Because of this, I would return to Mil

estone at least a few more times.
