REVIEWER: Jon Doughty


NAME: Poke Chef


ADDRESS: 580 California Blvd


CITY: San Luis Obispo


FOOD: 4


SERVICE: 4


VENUE: 4


RATING: 4


WRITTEN REVIEW: 


Poke Chef just open fairly recently here in San Luis Obispo. They are located

right next to Boba Stop in the little corner area right off the California 

exit as you are headed south on 101. The place is very small with just a few

indoor tables and a few outdoor tables as well.


Upon entering, all the staff were very friendly and greeted me with a smile.

They then asked if it was my first time (which it was) and informed me how

their ordreing system words. The options are varying size bowls or a wrap

which I though was interesting for an asian-fusion restaurant. The next step is

to pick all the items that you are interested in and they add them together for

you.


I order a small bowl, since I had never tried poke before with a brown rice for

the base. I went with the salmon and spicy tuna. For vegetables I added carrots

and edamame. I then tried the spicy wasabi sauce which was very good and had

them add that as well. My other toppings included seaweed salad and pickled

ginger. The staff was friendly and willing to make suggestions and what

pair better with certain things so you don't end up with a bowl of conflicting

flavors.


In the end, I thought the fish was fresh and tender, which was probably the

most important part to me. Everything ended up tasting good together. The price

was $10 for a small bowl which is kinda pricey, but worth the fresh healthy

ingredients.
