Raku Ramen in San Luis Obispo is a Ramen bar that also sells rice bowls and tempura. The price of the food was pretty fair for the amount you get and is pretty high quality. It was served pretty quickly and not hot enough that you had to wait to eat.

When we first entered the staff offered us menus and was very friendly when we asked them questions about the menu. Once you are done ordering they give a device to indicate that the food was ready. They were very friendly when retrieving the food.

The venue is kind of small but has a parking lot behind the building, making it very accessible for downtown slo. The layout of the building makes walking through the tables with bowls of hot soup a little bit hard.

Raku is a really great ramen house in downtown slo. It is accessible and makes a pretty good bowl of noodles. If you have a craving for noodles and want to have them pretty quickly this is a great spot to go.
