REVIEWER: Joey Wilson

NAME: Chili Peppers

ADDRESS: 2121 Broad St

CITY: San Luis Obispo

FOOD: 3

SERVICE: 4

VENUE: 2

RATING: 3

WRITTEN REVIEW: 

Chili Peppers is a Mexican restaurant that serves the standard assortment of burritos, tacos, and other common Mexican dishes. I ordered a chicken burrito and was full when I was done eating it. The burrito was not as spicy I would have liked.

The service was nice at Chili Peppers. They took the order and the food was prepared right in front of you and quickly. I was happy with the service.

Chili Peppers was not a visually pleasant restaurant. It seemed a little bit dirty. I did like how they have plants outside though.

Overall Chili Peppers was decent food. You will be full when you leave but not super satisfied. I would suggest Taqueria Santa Cruz instead.
