REVIEWER: Gavin Scott

NAME: Fattoush

ADDRESS: 5 Santa Rosa Street

CITY: San Luis Obispo

FOOD: 4

SERVICE: 3

VENUE: 3

RATING: 4

WRITTEN REVIEW: 

The food is good for the price; you can get a big portion of good food for only around ten dollars. It does well compared to the other Mediterranean restaurants in town as well; the portions are much bigger and the food is as good, if not better.

The venue is pretty standard for the type of place it is. You order at the counter and pay when you're done, with standard cafeteria seating. The place is clean and neat, if nothing special. The parking situation is very poor, with the only available spots nearby in a crowded lot that is hard to navigate.

The service is pretty good, the food comes out very quickly and the staff is polite. Many of the people behind the counter are from the Mediterranean and have heavy accents as a result, which can make them hard to understand. However, this does not detract from the quality of the service very much.

Overall the restaurant is good , and I will likely go back in the future. Some of the food was much better than I expected, and much better than its competition, but the side-courses I tried were either not as good or not as generously-portioned as other similar restaurants I've been to in the area.
