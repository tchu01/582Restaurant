REVIEWER: Justin Postigo

NAME: Upper Crust Trattoria

ADDRESS: 11560 Los Osos Valley Road

CITY: San Luis Obispo

FOOD: 4

SERVICE: 5

VENUE: 4

RATING: 4

WRITTEN REVIEW:

Upper Crust Trattoria is an Italian restaurant. Upper Crust Trattoria is located off of Los Osos Valley Road. It is near other restaurants like Laguna Grill, Sushiya, and Burger King. Upper Crust Trattoria shares a parking lot with Laguna Grill so there were no issues with finding a place to park. The food is a bit pricey, but I believe the cost of the food reflects the quality very nicely. 

Upper Crust Trattoria is great if you want to sit down and have a nice dinner. The food quality is above average. There is a wide variety of menu items to choose from whether it be pasta, pizza, seafood, and other meats. The service is also above average because the people there are very polite and kind. They always check up on you to make sure you are happy. The food preparation did not take long at all. The cost of the food varies depending on what you order. Appetizers are around $10 each, while pasta dishes are about $15 and the entrees are around $20. 

For appetizers, there are many good choices such as the stuffed mushrooms, scallops, and calamari. There are also many different kinds of pasta dishes that have chicken, beef, and seafood. Some of the entrees include filet mignon, chicken marsala, and veal scaloppini. I believe the cost of the food reflects the quality and quantity of the food. The atmosphere is above average. Inside, it looks like an authentic restaurant in Italy. There is a fireplace, with fake fire, ceiling lamps, candles, and wooden tables and chairs. It was a very comfortable place to eat at. After your meal, you can enjoy one of their many desserts such as cannolis, cheesecake, and espresso. The overall experience is above average because the quality food, friendly service, and fair prices. I would recommend this restaurant to people who are looking for a nice place to sit down and enjoy Italian-style food.

Upper Crust Trattoria has a connected parking lot with other restaurants. I did not have any trouble with parking but on a busy day, I can see how it might prove difficult to find parking. It is right off of Los Osos Valley Road and there are many other places nearby such as Grocery Outlet, so you can do some shopping after having lunch or dinner. I would definitely come back and eat here again with some of my family or friends and I would recommend this place for people who have not yet tried it.
