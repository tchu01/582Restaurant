REVIEWER: Sage Maxwell


NAME: Blaze Pizza


ADDRESS: 892 E. Foothill Boulevard #5A


CITY: San Luis Obispo


FOOD: 5


SERVICE: 4


VENUE: 4


RATING: 4


WRITTEN REVIEW: 


Blaze pizza is a unique restaurant. It is best described as a pizza themed subway. You are able to build your pizza as it goes down a line of ingredients.


The ingredients are great. As someone who is allergic to both gluten and eggs I was delighted to find out their gluten-free dough is also vegan. For the price of two dollars extra I can enjoy pizza again which is exciting. 


When asking for the allergen hand book the person wiped down every laminated page before giving to me. I thought it was a nice gesture in cause I was super allergic to anything. They don't get a 5 star rating because they misspelled my name. Shee is not Sage, though I can see why they would make this misspelling. Went back a second time because it was so tasty. They misspelled it again. This time they spelled it "Shage". Amazing


Their outdoor seating is excellent. The seating although small is very comfortable. They have a nice little fake fire with cool fake gems, that 10 year old me would have definitely stolen a couple of. Overall this restaurant is very nice. The food quality is great, price is reasonable and everything feels new.
