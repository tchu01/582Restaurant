REVIEWER: Joey Wilson

NAME: Fatoush

ADDRESS: 5 Santa Rosa St

CITY: San Luis Obispo

FOOD: 4

SERVICE: 5

VENUE: 2

RATING: 4

WRITTEN REVIEW: 

Fatoush has good Mediterranean food. I got a gyro plate and it was delicious. It came with a lot of food. I really liked the pita bread that came with the plate.

The service was great. The employee's were friendly and came out to talk to us while we ate. The food was prepared quickly and was fresh.

The venue is alright. Ordering is done at a outdoor window which was not very nice. They have some patio seating but it is next to a busy road. This place is more geared for takeout.

Fatoush has good Mediterranean food at a fair price. The employees are friendly and good. The location could be better. Overall Fatoush is a nice place to eat.
