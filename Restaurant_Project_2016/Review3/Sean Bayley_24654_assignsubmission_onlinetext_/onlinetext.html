REVIEWER: Sean Bayley

NAME: Taco de Mexico

ADDRESS: 374 Santa Rosa St

CITY:  San Luis Obispo

FOOD: 2.5

SERVICE: 5

VENUE: 4

RATING: 3

WRITTEN REVIEW: 

I've been on a search for an exceptional taco in SLO for years. I've driven by Taco de Mexico so many times, but I've never went in. Tonight I decided to give it a shot. I ordered a carne asada taco and an al pastor taco, with a negra modelo. Suffice it to say... the search is still ongoing. I really didn't enjoy either taco. They just don't have the same flavors that I've grown accustomed to at the numerous taco shops in san diego. If you aren't a taco snob, you might think this place is great. (the reviews on yelp are generally pretty good)

Apart from the food, I really enjoyed the overall experience. The service was great. The staff was friendly and helpful, and my food was brought out to me pretty quickly. From the moment I walked in until the moment I took my first bite, I thought that this might be the place.

The venue is classic. I mean it is really spot on. It's just a perfect taco shop feel. It's small but not too small, dirty but not too dirty... I really had high hopes for this place. 

Unfortunately, I won't be coming back to Taco de Mexico. The food just doesn't justify the price -- I paid close to $10 for two tacos and a beer. As sad as it is to say... Chillie Peppers has better food than this place AND is a better deal. Tacos de Acapulco still has the best in town, IMO.
