REVIEWER: christian skylar durst


NAME: Golden China Restaurant


ADDRESS: 7425 El Camino Real


CITY: Atascadero


FOOD: 3


SERVICE: 4


VENUE: 4


RATING: 3


WRITTEN REVIEW:


Golden China Restaurant is one of the only Chinese food restaurants this side of the grade. It features a buffet and a relaxed dining environment, although you could also order by the plate if you want to.


We went to the Golden China for its Dinner Buffet, which was the only recommended time to go there based on its Yelp and Google Reviews. Thankfully, the food was pretty warm and fresh. There was a very wide selection of appetizers, entr√©es and desserts - mostly focused around fried meats. We each got a healthy sampler of different items. The food was a little greasy and salty, and i would have preferred more spicy options. Dessert was sub-par; it was mostly composed of bulk dessert options that would all be found at your local grocer.


When i imagine an all-you-can-eat buffet, i don't really expect actual servers. However, Golden China had very prompt servers and our drinks were constantly refilled and he checked in on us often. When paying, i had accidentally forgotten to bring my coupon, but he fixed it for me, which was a pleasant surprise. i let him have the discount as part of his tip.


The Golden China was pretty easy to find and had ample parking, which is unusual in Atascadero. While the entry area is an odd-looking pink bar, the actual dining area was clean and had some novel presentation elements such as decorated tables and plates. In addition, my partner and i were able to sit and chat for about an hour without being asked to leave, despite it being somewhat busy.


There's not a lot of American-Chinese restaurants in Atascadero, and Golden China fulfills that niche. If you're looking for something more noodle-focused or other vegetarian options, you'll need to go somewhere else. In addition, it isn't the most cheap place. Make sure you bring your coupon!
