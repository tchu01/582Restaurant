WRITTEN REVIEW:

Black Sheep Bar and Grill is a small hole in the wall pub in downtown San Luis Obispo. It has amazing mac and cheese where you can choose what you want in it. It has made a name for itself from this mac and cheese but also has burgers and other pub food. It also has a decently priced alcohol menu with a good selection.

If you aren't sitting at the bar sometimes it is hard to get your servers attention. It seems lively and fun at the bar but if you're sitting at a table there is sometimes a wait to get noticed. They are really good about getting your food and drinks to you but getting their attention away from the busy bar is sometimes hard.

The venue is pretty small with outside seating and limited table space. At times the bar can smell like tobacco smoke which is a personal turn off for me but is overall nice and comfortable.

Overall Black Sheep Bar and Grill is definitely worth a try. The mac and cheese is a must and it's conveniently located right next to one of the parking garages.
