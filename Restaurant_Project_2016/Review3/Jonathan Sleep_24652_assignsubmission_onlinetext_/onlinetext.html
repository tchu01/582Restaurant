WRITTEN REVIEW:

Aisuru Sushi Bar is a sushi restaurant in downtown San Luis Obispo. To order food you sit down at a table and order food. The food was pretty good. I ordered 4 Ninja rolls because they have a 2-for-1 special on Monday nights. The crab was definitely imitation but I doused it in soy sauce and it was fantastic.

The service was great. Our waiter was attentive and he brought out the drinks and food promptly. He also explained menu items and checked up on us in regular intervals.

The venue was sufficient. It had plenty of inside seating and TV screens to watch whatever sports game was on. The location was located in downtown SLO which is very convenient. I was able to park right outside.

Overall, I had a good experience at Aisuru. There 2-for-1 Ninja roll deal on Mondays are pretty hard to beat. If youâ€™re looking for high end sushi, this will not be it.
